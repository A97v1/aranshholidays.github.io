Day 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing

After relax, cover Srinagar sightseeing Mughal gardens like Nishat Garden, Shalimar Garden, Chashma Shahi, Pari Mahal / Shakar Acharya Temple and late in the evening you can opt for a Shikara Ride(at your own) on the famous Dal lake and back to hotel for a comfortable overnight stay in hotel

After relax, enjoy famous natural surroundings and visit Chandanwadi, Aru, Betaab Valley (on your own payment There is an entry fee of Rs 25 /- per head to be paid at the entrance of the valley. Here also you will be given one hour to go around the valley area by your cab driver). In the evening back to Hotel for a comfortable overnight stay in Pahalgam Hotel.
