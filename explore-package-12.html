Day 1: MANALI (OVERNIGHT TRAVEL) (570 Kms)

At 05: 00 pm reports at Delhi Volvo pick up point (on your own) to Depart for Manali (Altitude 6727ft). Overnight Journey by Volvo

Day 2: LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

Reach Manali at 6 - 7 am , Meet our TEAM MEMBER at Manali Volvo Stand and transfer to the hotel. After fresh up till 2, proceed to half day city tour of Manali which includes Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashsisht Hot Springs, Tibeten Monastry, Club house. Dinner & Overnight Stay

Day 3: JISPA/SARHU

Today at 10 am drive to Sarchu (Altitiude 10500 ft) in Lahual. At 4 pm reach Sarchu & Check into the Camp. Rest of the day free for rest and independent activities. Dinner & Overnight Stay.

Day 4: LEH (340 KMS)

At 6 am check out from the hotel and drive to Leh (Altitude 11, 562 ft) . Arrive Leh by 6 pm and check into the hotel. Spend the day at your leisure. Dinner & Overnight Stay.

Day 5: LEH LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

After breakfast at 9 am, Have Leh City tour covering: Shey Palace, Thiksey, Hemis monasteries, Stok Palace Museum.and Sindhu ghat (one of the seven most sacred rivers in India). Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace & market. Overnight Stay in Hotel.

Day 6: NUBRA VALLEY (40 kms)

After Breakfast, at 10:00 am check out from the hotel and drive to Nubra Valley via Khardungla pass (The Highest Motorable road in the World, 18,380 ft) .Arrive at Deskit by 12 noon. At 5 pm explore Deskit, Hunder Villages and camel Safari in Sand Dunes between Deskit and Hunder Village. Overnight in Camp.

Day 7: LEH

After Breakfast visit Deskit Monastery and drive back to Leh by same Route, crossing Khardungla Pass. Overnight Hotel.

Day 8: PANGONG LAKE

After Breakfast, drive to Pangong Lake. Reach on the Banks of pangong Lake through Changla Pass (At Altitude 17,350 ft & 3rd highest motorable road in the world). On arrival appreciate the changing Colors and high altitude of the Lake (situated at 4,270 ft. & 134 km long which freezes completely during winter, despite being saline water. Later Drive back to Leh by the same route. Overnight Hotel.

Day 9: MANALI JOURNEY (477 kms)

After Breakfast, depart for Manali at 6 am. At 9 pm reach Manali & check into the hotel. Dinner & Overnight Hotel.
