Day 1: Arrival - Goa

As soon as you reach Goa, our team member will greet and escort you to the hotel. Check-in to the hotel and relax. Remainder of the day is at leisure. Experience a laid back tropical vibe on this day. Go shopping in the local markets and savor authentic delights. Towards the end of the day, come back to the hotel for a restful sleep.

Day 2: North Goa

A delectable breakfast begins your day, followed by a day trip to North Goa to visit its historical treasures and have a gala time at its beaches. Visit Aguada Fort and Calangute Annexe. Proceed to Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, and Anjuna Beach. After enjoying a great day as per your North Goa sightseeing package, come back to the hotel and slip into your bed for a soothing sleep.

Arise to a nutritious breakfast and get ready to head to the Grand Island for a memorable experience. Reach Coco Beach jetty and reach Grand Island via boat ride.
