Day 1: Cochin to Munnar Journey (130 Kms )

Welcome to Queen of Arabian Sea! Our transporter will warmly greet you at Cochin Airport/ Railway Station and transfer you to pre-booked hotel in Munnar. On the way take a halt at Cheeyappara & Vallara Falls. The abundant greenery and gushing waterfalls will surely delight your heart. Reach Munnar & check into the hotel. Rest of the day will be free for leisure activities. Overnight Stay will be in hotel.

Day 2: Munnar Local Sight Seeing

After breakfast embark for an interesting tour to Munnar town. Places to cover will be Photo Point, Jungle Honey Bee Nest, Madupetty Dam/Lake, DTPC Boating, Elephant Arrival Spot, Cinema Shooting Point, Echo Point, Gundala Lake, Spice Plantation, Pothamedu Sunset View. Return and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay in hotel.

Day 3: Munnar to Thekkady Journey time :(110KM)

Enjoy your breakfast, check out from the hotel and then it is time to hit the road to Thekkady, which is a perfect getaway to take a peek into the wild-side of Nature. Reach the destination and check into hotel. Later take a stroll in Spice Plantations of Thekkady & enjoy a boat ride over Periyar Lake. Overnight stay will be in Hotel.

Day 4: Thekkady to Kumarakom Journey Time (120KM)

Our next destination will be Kumarakom. So pack up your bags and leave for the location. On the way get the real essence of Kerala ‚Äì Colourful Floras, Emerald Tea Plantations and Beautiful Waterfalls will be definitely treat to eyes. Upon reaching Kumarakom check into the hotel and enjoy a lavish stay.

Day 5: Kumarakom√¢ to Alleppey Journey (45KM) Houseboat

After breakfast check out from the hotel and proceed to Alleppey. Stay arrangements will be at traditional Kerala houseboat. Cruise along the backwaters of Alleppey and take a closer look at simple lifestyle of residents. Also enjoy traditional meals onboard while admiring the views of Paddy fields and surrounding islands. Overnight stay will be in houseboat.

Day 6: Alleppey-Kovlam JOURNEY TIME (160 Kms)

After a delightful morning meal, deboard the houseboat and proceed to Kovalam. En-route Capture the endless sight of Coconut trees and lovely landscapes. Arrive at the destination & transfer to the hotel. Overnight stay in hotel.

Today amuse yourself with the full day sightseeing of Trivandrum. Interesting places to cover will be Trivandrum Zoo, Napier Museum, Chitra Art Gallery, Shree Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Veli Village. Later it is time to return to hotel for overnight stay.
