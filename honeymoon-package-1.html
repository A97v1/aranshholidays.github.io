Day 1: Cochin To Munnar

Welcome to Queen of Arabian Sea! Our transporter will warmly greet you at Cochin Airport/ Railway Station and transfer you to pre-booked hotel in Munnar. On the way take a halt at Cheeyappara & Vallara Falls. The abundant greenery and gushing waterfalls will surely delight your heart. Reach Munnar & check into the hotel. Rest of the day will be free for leisure activities. Overnight Stay will be in hotel.

Day 2: Local Sight Seeing

After breakfast embark for an interesting tour to Munnar town. Places to cover will be Photo Point, Jungle Honey Bee Nest, Madupetty Dam/Lake, DTPC Boating, Elephant Arrival Spot, Cinema Shooting Point, Echo Point, Gundala Lake, Spice Plantation, Pothamedu Sunset View. Enjoy a comfortable overnight Stay in hotel.

Day 3: Munnar - Thekkady

Enjoy your breakfast, check-out from the hotel and then it is time to hit the road to Thekkady, which is a perfect getaway to take a peek into the wild-side of Nature. Reach the destination and check into the hotel. Later take a stroll in Spice Plantations of Thekkady and enjoy a boat ride over Periyar Lake. Overnight stay will be in Hotel.

Day 4: Thekkady to Kumarakom

Our next destination will be Kumarakom. So pack-up your bags and leave for the location. On the way get the real essence of kerala ‚Äì Colorful Floras, Emerald Tea Plantations and Beautiful Waterfalls, which will definitely be a treat to your eyes. Upon reaching Kumarakom check into the hotel and enjoy a lavish stay.

Day 5: Alleppey-Cochin

After breakfast check-out from the hotel and proceed to Alleppey. Stay arrangements will be at traditional Kerala Houseboat. Cruise along the backwaters of Alleppey and take a closer look at simple lifestyle of residents. Also enjoy traditional meals on-board while admiring the views of Paddy fields and surrounding Islands. Overnight stay will be in houseboat.
