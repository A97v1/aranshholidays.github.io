Romantic Honeymoon Tour Delhi Manali

Location: Andes mountain range in the south of Peru

Begin your forever love story with a breathtaking journey from Delhi to the dreamy hills of Manali.

Description

Manali is located at a height of around 6398 feet on the northern end of the Kullu Valley, Manali is one of the best getaway destinations in the country. Ranging from verdant rolling plains, breathtaking passes, scenic landscapes replete with snow-clad mountains, meadows of pine, and cascading waters offering spectacular views and a chance to cherish natural beauty at its best. Manali honeymoon tour package is one of the most relaxing and enjoyable Himachal honeymoon packages. This Manali honeymoon package from Delhi by volvo takes care of relaxation, sightseeing, and a little adventure. it takes you from Delhi to Manali by Volvo. From there, you go to Rohtang, Kullu and Manikaran. Often referred as the home to the Seven Sages, this place is dotted with various temples and other revered shrines. Visit Hadimba Devi Temple and monasteries, set amidst scenic beauty.

Day 1: Delhi - Manali Overnight AC-Semi Sleeper Volvo Bus

Start the trip by boarding the Volvo from Delhi in the evening. It will be an overnight journey which will help you in rejuvenating & keeping you ready for the fun filled trip ahead. Do carry your eatables or have on the way.

Day 2: Manali Local Sightseeing

Reach Manali bus stand in the morning and transfer will be arranged to the Hotel. Freshen up, have Tea and proceed to explore Manali city.The main tourist attractions in Manali include Mall Road, Club House, Tibetan monasteries, Hadimba Temple, Vashisth hot water spring & temple, and much more to explore. Return to the hotel and have a comfortable night's sleep. Accommodation, Sight seeing, Morning Tea and Dinner included on this day

Day 4: Manali to Kullu & Manikaran

Get set for another day to create life long memories and proceed to Kullu. Enjoy River Rafting, Paragliding and on the way buy some beautiful Kullu shawls. Head towards prettily situated Manikaran at an elevation of 1,850mtr, in the lap of the magnificent Parbati valley. Well known for its Gurudwara and pretty temples, it is a famous pilgrim centre of both Hindus and Sikhs. Manikaran is famed for its hot water springs. The gushing water is so hot that one can prepare ood in it. The water in the springs is radioactive and so it also has got some medicinal value. After returning to hotel have dinner and comfortable night sleep. Accommodation, Sightseeing, Breakfast and Dinner included on this day.

Day 5: Departure Back to Delhi

Relish delicious breakfast and check out of the hotel. The rest of the day is all yours. Enjoy, explore and catch your Volvo bus back to Delhi on time. With an overnight journey the trip ends with fabulous memories to cherish lifelong.
