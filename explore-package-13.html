Day 1: GOA ARRIVAL

Upon arrival at Goa Railway Station /Airport get greeted by our team member who will transfer you to the pre-booked Hotel/Resort. Evening free to stroll on the beaches & chill out at the beach shacks. Overnight stay in Hotel. .

Day 2: NORTH GOA TOUR

After breakfast depart for North Goa Tour. Places to cover in North Goa will be Coco beach, Fort Aguada, Dolphin Trip (On your own), Vaga Beach, Anjuna Beach, Calangute Beach. By late evening return to hotel for Overnight stay.

Day 3: SOUTH GOA TOUR

Today we will be heading on towards South Goa. Places to cover in South Goa will include visits to finest heritage places in Goa. We will start off with Basilica of Bom Jesus, A World heritage site which holds the remains of St. Francis Xavier. Thereafter you will have a choice to either explore Museum of Christian Art or Wax World Museum near Gandhi Circle. Our trip will continue to Mangueshi & Shantu Dugra Temple. At last, our South Goa sightseeing will finish off with a visit to Ancestral Goa ‚Äì A recreational yet educational spot at Loutolim . Late evening return to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 4: DUDHSAGAR WATERFALL DAY TOUR

Have a heartily breakfast leave for excursion trip to Dudhsagar waterfall. This waterfall is well acclaimed for its milk colored streams flowing fiercely through the lush green panoramas on the borders of Goa. Another reason to visit this place is its ranking among one of the hundred highest waterfalls in the world. After rejoicing your time near Dudhsgar Waterfall proceed back to hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 5: DEPARTURE

Today morning after breakfast check out from the hotel and you will be dropped at airport/railway station for your further journey............
