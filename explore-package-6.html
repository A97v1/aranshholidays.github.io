Day 1: ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR

Today is the day to start off your Rajasthan tour Package . Arrive at Jaipur Airport/Railway station, meet our representative & transfer to the hotel. Later cover Albert Hall(Museum) , Birla Mandir , Hawa Mahal , Overnight stay at Hotel

Day 2: JAIPUR SIGHTSEEING

At 10 am have Full Day Jaipur City Tour: City Palace Complex, Jantar Mantar (Stone Observatory), Hawa Mahal, Jal Mahal, Amber Fort . Overnight stay at Hotel

Day 3: JAIPUR - JODHPUR

After breakfast, depart from Jaipur to JODHPUR . On arrival in Jodhpur , Check into the hotel. Evening explore local market by own .Overnight stay at hotel

Day 4: JODHPUR SIGHTSEEING
