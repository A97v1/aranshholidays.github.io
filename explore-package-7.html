Day 1: Destination : Jaipur

Day 2: Destination : Jaipur Sightseeing

Today we will be have Jaipur Local sightseeing: Cover City Palace Complex, Jantar Mantar (Stone Observatory) Jal Mahal, Amber Fort, Nalagarh Fort, Jaigarh Fort . Overnight stay at Hotel
